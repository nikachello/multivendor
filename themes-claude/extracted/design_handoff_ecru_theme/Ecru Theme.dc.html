Material study

Why we keep coming back to linen

Spun from flax that needs little water and no irrigation, linen gets cooler as the day gets hotter and softer with every wash. It creases — and we think that’s the point.

Our European flax is woven at a mill running on renewable power, then finished without optical brighteners.
